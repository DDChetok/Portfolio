Monte-Carlo Tree Search for Checkers

Do yo think you can defeat a master of Checkers?

This is an AI who is able to play checkers at a competitive level. It’s based on “Monte-Carlo Tree Search”, an AI algorithm which is used by the most powerful AI in games, AlphaZero. You can choose different game modes: Human vs Human, Human vs AI, AI vs AI and Sandbox. You can choose the difficult of the AI through the number of simulations of the MCTS algorithm (the more simulations, the more difficult). Also you can select the dimensions of the board, being able to play Checkers in a 20x20 board.

You can try to win against this AI here!

  • Date: May 2020
  • Project: Final degree project
  • Job: Develop the entire project by myself
  • Platform: Web Browser

Tale of Jade

Defeat the emperor using your extraordinary abilities

Tale of Jade is a metroidvania set in medieval China. In the game you control Shui Qui, one of the rebels who has been blessed by the gods to defeat the emperor. You have different abilities, which will be unlocked during the game to complete this mission. Some of them are:

  • Push: You can use it to push enemies far away, throw stage elements at them or use it against a wall to make big jumps.
  • Pull: You can attract enemies, objects or use it against a wall to move in the scenario like using lianas in a jungle.
  • Raise: Use it for create a rock from the ground and combine it with other abilities to defeat your enemies.

Snapped

Have you ever thought about playing a battle royale in the real life?

Snapped is a multiplayer location-based battle royale game for portable devices. They are two types of player: the one who is the “refree”, who is called the GameMaster(GM) and the “normal” player. The “normal” player has to play like in other battle royale games, hiding from other players and using the camera in his device to make photos to other players in order to kill them. These photos are sended to the GM, who decides if one player has killed the other one. The GM has to set up the game, delimite the battleground (wich is in the real world), control its size and when it will become smaller, send drops with ammo to the players and deciding if a photo wich has received is good enough to kill the other player.

If you want to play with your friends you can play it on the browser or download it here!

The project is visible for everyone in GitHub

  • Date: December 2019
  • Project: Final project for “Web Videogames” subject
  • Job: multiplayer and Gameplay programmer
  • Platform: Mobile/Tablet/Web Browser

Neon Beat

Play a music game with any audio file you can imagine

Neon Beat is a procedural rythm game in which the level you play is the wave an audio’s frequencies generate. Even though there are three predefined songs, you can upload any mp3 file you have in your phone or comuter. That mp3 can be anything! The song you hear every morning, a whatsapp audio your friend sended to you last night drunk at 4 a.m, etc. The player is blue ball who travels along the wave. The white balls in the wave are “control points”. The player has to tap the screen (phone) or press the spacebar/left mouse click (pc) to score them. If the player taps too early or too late, he fails and he loose hp. The color of the player represents his current hp, if you are red you are in troubles.

You can play here!

  • Platform: Mobile/Tablet/Web Browser

Brainstein

Save the world protecting the Einstein's brain with your scientist friend!

Brainstein is a multiplayer cooperative game in wich you and your partner, the two greatest scientists on Earth, have to survive to infinite zombie waves. Every player has 3 weapons: the pistol, with unlimited ammo, the shotgun wich fires 3 shots in area, and the AK-47, wich has the biggest fire rate. Also the players have to protect the Einstein’s brain wich teleports randomnly every round. At the end of the round drops appear in the map so the players can have more ammo.

Brainstein is a web game and the comunication between players is by a server in STS.

Environment AI Simulation

Experiment how a predator and his prey evolve in a procedural

Complete autonomus simulation of an envirorment where lives 2 species of animals, rabbits and foxes. These two species are programmed using the same behaviour tree, but producing different types of behaviours. Rabbits eat bushes while foxes eat rabbits. All of them can be hungry, thirsty, pregnant (if they are female) or running away from their predators (only rabbits have a predator, the foxes). Children are born with a random combination of the stats of their parents. Moreover the scenario is generated programmatically using parameters given by the user, so it produces an infinite map generator.

The project is visible for everyone in GitHub

  • Date: December 2019
  • Project: Final project for “Character behaviour” subject
  • Job: AI programmer
  • Platform: PC
